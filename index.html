Oyam District Northern Uganda

Oyam District is located in the Northern Region of Uganda. It is a landlocked country known for its diverse landscapes, which include the Rwenzori Mountains and Lake Victoria. The district, predominantly rural, focuses heavily on agriculture. Uganda is often referred to as the "Pearl of Africa" due to its natural beauty and rich biodiversity.

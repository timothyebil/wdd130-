About Us

Our Mission

At Dry Oar Rafting, our mission is to deliver safe, exhilarating, and unforgettable whitewater adventures. We believe in connecting people with the raw beauty of nature while promoting river stewardship and ensuring the highest safety standards for all adventurers.

Our History

Founded in 2010 by a group of passionate river guides, Dry Oar Rafting started with just three rafts and a dream to share the thrill of the rapids. Over the last 15 years, we have grown into a premier rafting company, known for our expert guides, top-tier equipment, and personalized customer service. We have navigated hundreds of miles of river, creating lasting memories for thousands of guests.

Why Choose Dry Oar Rafting?

Safety is our top priority. Our team consists of certified, highly trained, and knowledgeable guides who know the rivers better than anyone. We ensure that every trip is customized to the experience level of our guests, from beginners to seasoned adventurers.

  • Experienced Certified Guides
  • Premium Quality Equipment
  • Small Group Sizes
  • Commitment to Safety & Fun

Adventure Awaits You!

Whether you are a beginner looking for a scenic float or an adrenaline junkie seeking Class IV rapids, Dry Oar Rafting has the perfect trip for you. Explore our popular journeys along the scenic Canyon River and the thrilling Rapids of Fortune.
